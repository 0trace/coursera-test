Responsive Design

Site that is designed to adapt to its layout to the viewing environment by using fluid, proportion-based grid, flexible images, and CSS3 media queries.
Site's layout adapts to the size of the device.
Content verbosity or its visual delivery may change.
There are alternatives to responsive design.

12-Column Grid Responsive Layout. Because of the factors of 12. We can split this Grid to 2, 3, 4, 6, 12 columns, depending on the size of the device. Or we can use nested 12-column grids in the divided colums of the main grid.
